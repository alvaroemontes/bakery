Born in Miami

Our Story

Where it all began

From a Small Kitchen
in Little Havana

Tumi started as a weekend project — a few trays of croissants, a family recipe for tres leches, and a folding table at the local farmers market. Our founder, raised between Miami and Lima, grew up surrounded by the layered flavors of two cities, and wanted to bring that warmth into every bake.

What began as a dozen pastries sold out by 8am became something we couldn't ignore. By 2021, we had our first kitchen. By 2023, our first storefront in Wynwood.

What Drives Us
Local & Seasonal
We source ingredients from South Florida farms and adjust our menu with the seasons.
Made from Scratch
Everything is baked fresh the morning it's sold. No day-old pastries. Ever.
Community First
We donate unsold baked goods nightly and partner with local nonprofits across Miami-Dade.
Today

Three Locations.
One Heart.

We now have three storefronts — Wynwood, Brickell, and South Beach — each with the same soul as that first farmers market table. Our team of 40+ bakers, baristas, and front-of-house staff are the backbone of everything we do.

Tumi isn't just a bakery. It's a gathering place for the people of Miami and everyone who visits.
